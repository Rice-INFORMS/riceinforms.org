What is DecisionLab?

DecisionLab is A semester-long program that provides hands-on, experiential learning opportunities through Decision Science and Data Science. Students apply Optimization, Simulation, AI, Stochastic Modeling, and other quantitative methods to tackle real-world challenges, thereby gaining teamwork experience.

Projects

In DecisionLab, students engage in tackling real-world problems, allowing them to witness the tangible impact of their efforts. Our projects fall into two categories:

  • Social Impact Projects: These projects may not offer immediate financial rewards or significant research acclaim (and thus are not as attractive to seasoned experts). However, they address pressing societal issues and provide invaluable learning opportunities for students looking to gain practical experience and bring substantial value for communities.
  • Entrepreneurial Ventures: Some projects within DecisionLab have the potential for monetization and growth, paving the way for startup ventures. These projects offer students the chance to develop their Minimum Viable Product (MVP) within a supportive environment.
